Credibility model

Five-star score built for cautious applicants:

Culture & safety

Signals from credible employee reviews, turnover warnings, and contractor treatment.

Work-life balance

Scheduling flexibility, async practices, realistic quotas, and paid/unpaid labor boundaries.

DEI & global access

Evidence that applicants outside the US/EU are accepted without nationality ambiguity.

Mobility & growth

Internal mobility, upskilling, repeat hiring, and pathways from contract to richer work.

Legal & pay hygiene

Employee-related lawsuit checks, wage clarity, payment reputation, and compliance posture.

Future

The current release is static and no-signup. The codebase keeps seams for profiles, saved jobs, moderator queues, and applicant notes.

  • Profiles: optional applicant preference storage.
  • Moderation: reviewer status and confidence logs.
  • Alerts: email or RSS only after the user opts in.

RITS is a free static prototype. Always verify job details on the employer/source site before applying.
